Youth Arts Festival

The Youth Arts Festival website will introduce the festival and showcase young musicians, poets, dancers, and digital artists. It will share information about events, workshops, photos, videos, and social media updates. The site will have a bright, creative design with simple navigation and will work well on phones, tablets, and computers, with accessible text and clear colour contrast.
